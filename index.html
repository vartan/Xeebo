Zeebo

Welcome to the Zeebo Team website! This website has been created for us to publicly keep track of the progress of our project. You may be asking yourself, what is Zeebo?

Zeebo is an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) being developed by students at California State University, Long Beach with the goal of competing in the AUVSI Robosub competition. In order to compete, Zeebo must be able to autonomously control itself underwater using vision systems and other awareness sensors. It will be required to perform a series of tasks suited to underwater robotics.
